ABOUT THE SITE

This is a site primarily for my worldbuilding and OCs! In a nutshell:

Manaweavers
A universe where magic takes the form of magical string that can be woven to create various effects. Pretty much all kinds of characters can exist, it's a pretty anything-goes sort of world design-wise. It's mostly urban fantasy, but there's sci-fi elements too. Focused mostly on Two, a guy who got decapitated and then revived as a vampire and is now investigating his own murder.
Periscope Systems
A sort of modern day scifi-ish universe. Sapient robots exist and were pretty much only highly-classified military projects until a few years ago. The titular company is a military contractor that was developing nanobot-based robots, one of which unfortunately escaped during development in the time where sapient robots—much less shapeshifting ones—were still unknown, so they kinda just quietly sent goons to attempt to reclaim it instead of revealing that they have the tech to create sapient shapeshifting robots. Focused mostly on said shapeshifting military robots, named Apoapsis and Periapsis.
Metalworks
A universe where magic can only be casted using stuff made from metal. Focuses on a group of college students researching magic. I'm still not sure on the name, I might change it eventually.
Hue Shift
Information on Chroma, a TV-headed robot who is also, unfortunately, a monster that has to eat people, and the world that surrounds it.
Argentum Antipodes
A historical fantasy world where vampires were revealed to the world by the popularization of color film photography.
Travelerverse
A post-apocalyptic fantasy world without any humans. There's also magic versions of modern everyday objects called Relics. I haven't made the page for it yet.
Sylvolaia
A fantasy world where a magical Fae Realm exists alongside a normal modern world. Its magic system is based on contracts with magical creatures that transform you, giving you traits of the magical creatures you make contracts with. It's my newest world, so lore is still in flux, and I've been making a lot of retcons.
Gamemaster
A TV-headed multiversal killing game host, with a silly attitude that doesn't match its content at all.
Ryva
My oldest world, a science-fantasy world with a magic system where you can basically do anything, as long as you can convince the god of magic that it's associated with one of the 6 elements of magic. I don't maintain its page any more, so I took it out of the navbar.

Also, text like this is able to be hovered over for additional information or sometimes just jokes. Some images are also hoverable, but I haven't chosen to mark this.
